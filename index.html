About Dr. Dasari

Dr. Vasantha Dasari is a highly experienced Obstetrician and Gynaecologist with over 10 years of experience. She is well-regarded for her commitment to women's health and her expertise in managing pregnancy and reproductive health issues. Dr. Vasantha is a dedicated Laparoscopic surgeon and fertility specialist, providing comprehensive care through personalized consultations and treatments. Her compassionate approach and ability to make patients feel at ease have made her a trusted partner for women navigating important stages in their lives. She is fluent in English, Telugu, Hindi, and Tamil, making her accessible to a diverse range of patients.
